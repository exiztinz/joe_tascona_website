About The Practice

Experienced workplace counsel with a focused, current presentation.

Joseph N. Tascona advises on labour and employment matters from Barrie, helping clients navigate severance, wrongful dismissal, labour relations, workplace safety, and related disputes.

Profile

More than 25 years assisting clients through difficult employment and labour disputes.

If you have been the victim of on-the-job injustice, Joseph N. Tascona is there to help. An experienced employment and labour lawyer based in Barrie, he has a focus on settling severance agreements, wrongful dismissal cases and employment contracts.

Joseph N. Tascona provides a number of legal services related to employment, including Canadian pension plans, disability benefits, health and safety prosecutions and labour relations board issues. Joseph N. Tascona has been helping workers in the Barrie and Lake Simcoe regions seek justice for more than 25 years. Call today if you require assistance with severance agreements, workplace compensation and wrongful termination cases.
